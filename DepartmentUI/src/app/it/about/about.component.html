Mechanical Engineering

About

Vision

To achieve excellence in the field of Mechanical Engineering and create competent professionals for the benefit of the world community.

Mission

  • M1: To integrate quality education, values, and skills in the field of Mechanical Engineering through an outcome-based teaching and learning process.

  • M2: To undertake research for the development of sustainable engineering solutions for real-world problems of society and industry.

  • M3: To foster an intellectual, innovative, and entrepreneurial environment to develop engineering leaders.

  • M4: To create a culture of collaboration and support among teachers and students for sharing ideas and knowledge in the field of Mechanical Engineering.

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

After few years of graduation, the graduates of Mechanical Engineering will be able to-
  1. Apply the acquired knowledge of Science, Mathematics, and Mechanical Engineering to pursue successful careers in industry, academia, and public service.
  2. Serve society and industry with engineering tools, management principles, and cutting-edge research for sustainable development in a collaborative mode.
  3. Exhibit professionalism by infusing leadership qualities, team spirit, effective communication skills, high ethical values, and empathy for the needs of society.
  4. Show commitment and engage in lifelong learning to enhance their professional and personal capabilities in the field of Mechanical Engineering.
