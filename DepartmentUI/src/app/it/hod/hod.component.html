Mechanical Engineering

HoD's Message

Dr. Dinesh Kumar Rao

Associate Professor & Head

Mechanical Engineering is the backbone of industrial progress, requiring innovation, precision, and timely solutions. The demand for professionals equipped with robust knowledge and skills in mechanical systems, materials, and processes led to the establishment of a formal UG level course in Mechanical Engineering. The aim of the department is to become a center of excellence in the field of Mechanical Engineering and related research, producing professionals who become leaders in innovation, entrepreneurship, and creativity in the engineering sector.
