Mechanical Engineering Innovation Lab
Established to advance research and development, our Mechanical Engineering Innovation Lab is equipped with cutting-edge tools and machinery. Facilities include 3D printers, CNC machines, a wind tunnel, material testing equipment, and CAD/CAM software. The lab collaborates with industries and research institutions, offering students hands-on experience with real-world projects, preparing them for successful careers in mechanical engineering.
