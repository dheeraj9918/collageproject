MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

Mechanical Engineering


The field of Mechanical Engineering encompasses numerous aspects of design, analysis, and manufacturing. The impact of technological advancements on human life is profound and widespread, particularly through the innovations brought forth by Mechanical Engineering. The Department of Mechanical Engineering is accredited by the NBA (National Board of Accreditation). Our department boasts highly skilled and experienced faculty members who are engaged in various research projects, pioneering new technologies. The department regularly conducts seminars and workshops on emerging topics. It aims to provide high-quality training to students using the latest advancements in Mechanical Engineering.

What should you expect from a degree in Mechanical Engineering at IET?-
  • Become a skilled engineer with strong ethical values.
  • Apply technical skills to solve societal problems.
  • Build a career as a design engineer, manufacturing engineer, or researcher.
  • Learn and apply project management concepts.
  • Develop soft skills for effective communication and presentations.
  • Campus placements at leading engineering companies.
  • Learn the latest engineering tools and technologies from internationally certified faculty.
  • Obtain certifications from recognized institutions like ASME, IEEE, and others.
  • Opportunities for student exchange programs at top global universities.
  • Participate in seminars, presentations, educational tours, expert lectures, internships, open electives, clubs, festivals, events, and more.
  • In-house classes by well-qualified faculty for GATE, GRE, and other competitive exams.
  • In addition to academics, we provide platforms to learn and get certified in foreign languages.

Mechanical Engineering Centre of Excellence
The Mechanical Engineering Centre of Excellence was established in collaboration with leading industry partners in January 2015. It is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including CNC machines, 3D printers, a wind tunnel, material testing equipment, and advanced CAD/CAM software. This centre aims to provide hands-on training and foster innovation in mechanical engineering through cutting-edge technology and industry-relevant projects.
